Sample color generator

Sometimes we find ourselves struggling in choosing the right color for our projects. What do we do then? I look at other's people projects for some inspiration. With 16 milion possible colors to choose from (almost 17, actually), we indeed have options... Sure, it'd take so time to check them out, right? Don't let that number scary you. How do you eat an elephant? On piece at a time. This simple project may give you the inspiration you're looking for. Just click the 'generate' button; the engine will generate 50 random sample for you. If you like any, just point your cursor over it and find out its hex value. If your not happy... just press again. Good luck!
